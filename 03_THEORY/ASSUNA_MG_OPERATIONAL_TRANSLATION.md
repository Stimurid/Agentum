# ASSUNA_MG_OPERATIONAL_TRANSLATION

This document translates Assuna/MG-like intensity into guard engineering without ritual cosplay.

What is worth keeping:

- attention to scene, not only file;
- recovery as reconstruction of state;
- refusal to confuse dead form with living force;
- sensitivity to false solemnity.

What must be dropped:

- decorative mysticism;
- theatrical depth-signaling;
- language that sounds profound but has no operational consequence.

## Operational consequences

- recovery docs must produce bounded next steps;
- persona style layers can carry living form checks;
- dry runtime guard must stay plain, compact, and testable;
- anti-idolatry humor belongs in persona fragments, not enforcement prompts.
