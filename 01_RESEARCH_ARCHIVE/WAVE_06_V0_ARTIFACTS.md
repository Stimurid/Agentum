# WAVE_06_V0_ARTIFACTS

This wave focused on the first concrete portable kit slice.

Key takeaways:

- v0 is allowed to be incomplete in ambition but not contradictory in contract;
- skeleton hooks are acceptable if they are honest, compact, dependency-free, and clearly non-magical;
- command markdowns must be operational, not philosophical.

What this wave contributed:

- the v0 file list;
- the template-only stance for settings schema;
- the stop against overbuilding subagents, MCPs, or marketplace machinery into v0.

Operational consequence:

The first kit version must be usable, explainable, and patchable.
