# WAVE_04B_BOUNDARIES_BRIDGES_QUINTA

This wave concentrated on project boundaries, bridge/export logic, and Quinta-like creative/workbench space.

Key takeaways:

- bridge/export is not generation;
- Quinta needs protection for invention, persona layers, and creative constraints, not flattening into a normal task manager;
- boundary mistakes often look reasonable until provenance is already lost.

What this wave contributed:

- WhiteCrow and Quinta profile distinctions;
- project-bleed sensitivity in shared infrastructure;
- separation between persona style fragments and dry runtime guard.

Operational consequence:

Do not let a bridge or creative system be reduced to generic folder semantics.
