# WAVE_04C_ASSUNA_MG_RECOVERY

This wave translated Assuna/MG-like recovery intuitions into operational guard language.

Key takeaways:

- recovery is not apology;
- recovery is not mystical recall theater;
- state reconstruction must name active project, loaded rules, evidence gap, and next bounded action;
- style without operational consequence becomes cosplay.

What this wave contributed:

- the recovery loader model;
- the anti-cosplay requirement in theory docs;
- the later `recover-agent` command shape.

Operational consequence:

If recovery cannot produce a bounded next step, it is still unfinished.
